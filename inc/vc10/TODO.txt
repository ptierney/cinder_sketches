
Fluid modeler - modeling and working with plastic forms


-	Interface should allow you to decide how the 
	molecules link together, eg Soda Constructor
-	Forms should be contained in a pen, thus allowing very
	liquid configurations.
-	User should be able to place forces of various types and 
	strengths around the "pen"
